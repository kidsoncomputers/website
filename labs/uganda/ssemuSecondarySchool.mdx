The Ssemu Secondary School in Jungwe Village, Mityana District, Uganda was established in June 2025. The lab consists of 15 desktops with Edubuntu, a laptop workstation with IIAB (Internet in a box), and a 65" TV for classroom presentations.
Ssemu Secondary school is a mixed boarding and day school serving about 600 students ages 13-20.
